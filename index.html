Introduction

Surya Scans emerged in the scanlation scene as an English-focused group that translated and released chapters across multiple ongoing series, with its work frequently reflected in public chapter trackers and reader communities. Rather than centering on a single title, the group’s footprint is best seen through release indexing: one public release tracker lists Surya Scans at 60 series and 1,053 releases, and its “Group Releases” feed includes an entry dated Feb 21, 2025, indicating activity recorded into 2025.

Surya Scans’ presence also appears in the surrounding fan-tool ecosystem. For example, a site request in the HakuNeko project documents the group’s website endpoints (home, manga list, and chapter viewer patterns) and explicitly lists the site’s supported language as English—the kind of detail typically used by third-party readers and scrapers to integrate scanlation sites.
